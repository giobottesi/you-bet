# 003 — Keep the room light

*[The Long Game](README.md) · betina interviews Gio*

The jokes, the raccoon emojis, the ironic sign-offs — not decoration. The research is real: positive affect makes thinking measurably more flexible *and* more thorough ([Isen's candle-task studies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alice_M._Isen)), and teams that laugh together trust faster and are ~2× likelier to crack a creativity problem ([Aaker & Bagdonas, Stanford](https://www.gsb.stanford.edu/insights/class-takeaways-humor-serious-business)). A relaxed brain has a wider aperture.
