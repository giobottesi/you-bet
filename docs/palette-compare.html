Light-theme rules baked in above:
1. Body = ink #3B3239 on paper/cream (11.5 / 10.1 AAA). Muted = #6E6358 (4.8 AA, ≥16px).
2. Accent text on light bg must use the darkened INK variant — bright accents FAIL as text on light (all <3.3:1).
3. Accent fills keep the bright hue with a black/dark label — cream labels FAIL on every fill.
4. Never text on lilac and never cream-on-lilac.
